PC Name
-------
1 -- 30.pic, with 30 being title

Amiga, Mac, and ST1 Names
----------
1 -- 29, with title being either title or 30

C64 Name
--------
pic(num), or "title mpic" for title

BBC Name
--------
"P.Title" or "title" for title pic
"P.Pic(num)" or "pic(num)" for others

CPC Name
--------
"title.pic", "1.pic" or "allpics.pic"

ST2 Name
--------
1 -- 30.squ, with 30 being title

If includes "title" -> pic 30
If "allpics" -> CPC picture set
Otherwise, pull number from end
